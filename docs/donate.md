# Donate to Friends of Courtenay 
Your gift helps fund basic needs, classroom resources, field trips, family events, and other experiences that public funding alone can’t cover.  

![A bright hallway at Courtenay](hallway.jpg "Courtenay 3rd graders quietly navigating the hallway")

## To give securely online

To support Friends of Courtenay, simply click the **Support today** button below. Online gifts are processed securely through our donation partner, and you can choose to make a one-time or recurring gift using a credit card, debit card, or checking account.

[Support today](https://19aid.com/courtenay-elementary-school-families-need-your-support/){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

## To give by check or stock

If you prefer to mail a check, give stock, or have other ideas for a donation, please email us and we’ll follow up with you.

[Email Friends of Courtenay](mailto:FriendsOfCourtenay@proton.me?subject=Donation%20inquiry){ .md-button .md-button--secondary }
